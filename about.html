ABOUT

Blackened post metal. Atmospheric Black Metal. Progressive Black Metal. Extreme metal.

Whatever you want to call them, no one can deny the dynamic existence of Vaelravyn. Marked by effortless shifts from ferocious tempest to graceful emotional devastation, this metal band from Nashville, TN never shies away from taking musical risks. They've curated a unique sound that is polished which remaining vulnerable and raw enough for even the most purist of metal listeners.
